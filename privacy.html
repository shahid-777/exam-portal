Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026 | Legal & Google OAuth Compliant

At Examination Terminal, we prioritize the confidentiality and security of our users' authentication data. This Privacy Policy outlines how your basic profile parameters are processed securely through Google OAuth and cloud nodes.

1. Information We Collect via Google Sign-In

When you authenticate utilizing Google Sign-In, we only query and store the minimal basic scope parameter identities required for academic verification: your **Full Name**, **Email Address**, and public **Profile Picture Token**. We do not request or access private contact lists, files, or background data.

2. How We Use Your Exam Data

Your fetched information is exclusively mapped within our secure infrastructure to:

  • Initialize secure temporary evaluation test sessions.
  • Identify and write candidate raw exam marks into the database results cluster.
  • Render authorized scores publicly onto the portal merit leaderboard.

3. Secure Data Storage Framework (Supabase Nodes)

All token registries and exam data states are hosted entirely inside secure, encrypted database environments managed by **Supabase**. No third-party data processing networks, commercial advertising groups, or external syndicates are granted reading layers over user performance files.

4. Local Storage & Cookies Utilization

The portal utilizes simple secure browser client caching hooks (`localStorage`) to retain specific session flags (like your userEmail string) active. This ensures your exam session stays persistent even if a terminal component drops, preventing instant quiz timeout logs.

5. User Data Deletion Rights

Students retain complete jurisdiction to request total deletion of their profile logs and historical evaluation record cards from our database. To wipe your active result nodes completely, a formal ticket can be processed with the technical campus system supervisor.

For further queries regarding secure cloud token parsing, you can consult the Main Portal System Administrator.
